LUXURIOUS HOTELS

LEELA PALACE


The Leela Palace is a quintessential symbol of luxury and grandeur in India, known for its opulent design, impeccable service, and fusion of traditional Indian architecture with contemporary elegance. Each property under the Leela Palace brand exudes the charm of royal palaces, offering a rich experience that reflects the cultural heritage and history of its location. The hotels are masterpieces of architectural beauty, featuring intricately carved facades, grand courtyards, and interiors adorned with fine art, luxurious textiles, and regal furniture that harken back to India’s royal past. Guests at The Leela Palace enjoy lavish rooms and suites, often with stunning views of their surroundings—whether it’s the serene Lake Pichola at The Leela Palace Udaipur or the bustling skyline of New Delhi. The rooms are spacious and meticulously designed, offering modern amenities alongside touches of traditional craftsmanship. The hotel’s culinary offerings are equally impressive, with gourmet restaurants serving a variety of cuisines, from Indian fine dining to international favorites, often helmed by world-renowned chefs. The Leela’s signature restaurant, Jamavar, is especially renowned for its authentic Indian flavors. In addition to luxury accommodations and fine dining, The Leela Palace properties are known for their world-class spas, such as the ESPA at The Leela Palace Udaipur, where guests can indulge in holistic wellness treatments inspired by both Indian and international practices. The hotels also boast state-of-the-art facilities, such as rooftop pools with panoramic views, private boat rides at lakeside locations, and lush landscaped gardens, providing guests with an immersive and serene environment. Overall, The Leela Palace delivers an unparalleled blend of tradition and modernity, offering guests a chance to experience the regal opulence of India’s royal palaces while enjoying the comforts and luxuries of contemporary living.

ONE AND ONLY REETHI RAH


One&Only Reethi Rah is a world-renowned luxury resort located in the Maldives, offering an exclusive and breathtaking tropical escape. Set on one of the largest private islands in the North Malé Atoll, the resort is surrounded by pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and lush tropical greenery. It is designed to provide the ultimate in privacy, relaxation, and luxury, with a blend of modern elegance and traditional Maldivian charm. The resort features spacious villas, either perched above the water or nestled along the beach, each designed with expansive indoor and outdoor spaces, private pools, and direct access to the sea or sandy shores. Every villa is carefully positioned to ensure privacy and offer stunning ocean views, making it the perfect retreat for those seeking tranquility and seclusion. One&Only Reethi Rah is celebrated for its personalized service and attention to detail, where every guest's needs are anticipated and catered to. Guests can enjoy a variety of world-class dining options, from gourmet international cuisine to fresh seafood prepared with local flavors. The resort’s restaurants are located in picturesque settings, such as beachside pavilions or overwater platforms, enhancing the dining experience with spectacular views of the Indian Ocean. The resort also offers private dining experiences on secluded beaches or aboard a luxury yacht for a truly intimate and romantic experience. In addition to its stunning accommodations and dining options, One&Only Reethi Rah provides an array of activities and experiences, including snorkeling and diving in the vibrant coral reefs, water sports, and private yacht excursions. The resort’s award-winning spa offers holistic wellness treatments inspired by both Eastern and Western practices, set in tranquil garden or overwater treatment rooms. The resort also has several infinity pools, a fitness center, and tennis courts, ensuring that guests can indulge in both relaxation and recreation.

BELMOND HOTEL CARUSO


Belmond Hotel Caruso is an exquisite luxury hotel perched high above the Amalfi Coast in the historic town of Ravello, Italy. Set within a magnificently restored 11th-century palace, the hotel embodies old-world charm and timeless elegance, offering panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the dramatic coastline below. The property’s architecture reflects its rich history, with vaulted ceilings, arched walkways, and beautifully landscaped terraced gardens filled with roses, olive trees, and lemon groves. Each room and suite is individually designed, blending classic Italian style with modern luxury, and many offer private terraces or balconies that overlook the shimmering blue waters of the Amalfi Coast. The highlight of Belmond Hotel Caruso is its stunning infinity pool, which seems to float above the sea, offering guests breathtaking, uninterrupted views of the coastline and the horizon beyond. This serene setting, combined with the hotel’s personalized service and attention to detail, creates a haven of tranquility and exclusivity. Dining at the hotel is a refined experience, with restaurants serving gourmet Italian cuisine made from the freshest local ingredients, often enjoyed on terraces that overlook the Mediterranean. Guests can indulge in regional delicacies and sip fine wines while taking in the unparalleled beauty of their surroundings. Beyond its luxurious accommodations, Belmond Hotel Caruso offers a wide range of curated experiences. Guests can explore the cultural and natural wonders of the Amalfi Coast with private boat trips, guided tours to nearby historical sites, or leisurely walks through Ravello’s charming streets. The hotel also offers a full-service spa, where guests can rejuvenate with holistic treatments inspired by the region’s natural resources, all while enjoying views of the coast from the treatment rooms. Belmond Hotel Caruso is not just a hotel but a destination in itself, where history, beauty, and luxury come together to create a truly magical experience.

THE PLAZA


The Plaza Hotel in New York City is an iconic symbol of luxury, sophistication, and timeless elegance, located at the corner of Fifth Avenue and Central Park South. Since its opening in 1907, The Plaza has been a hallmark of opulence, attracting royalty, celebrities, dignitaries, and discerning travelers from around the world. The hotel's striking French Renaissance-inspired architecture and grand interiors reflect its historic legacy, with a blend of old-world charm and modern luxury. Its spacious guest rooms and suites are designed with lavish décor, featuring crystal chandeliers, rich fabrics, and opulent marble bathrooms, many offering breathtaking views of Central Park or the Manhattan skyline. Beyond its luxurious accommodations, The Plaza offers a range of world-class amenities and services. The Palm Court, with its iconic stained-glass dome, is renowned for its elegant afternoon tea, offering a refined experience in a beautiful setting. The hotel’s fine dining options include The Rose Club and The Champagne Bar, where guests can enjoy gourmet cuisine and expertly crafted cocktails in chic, sophisticated surroundings. The Plaza also houses The Todd English Food Hall, an upscale dining venue offering a variety of gourmet culinary options. The Plaza is deeply intertwined with the cultural fabric of New York City, having been featured in numerous films, books, and television shows. It has become a symbol of Manhattan’s grandeur and history, serving as a backdrop to countless legendary events. Guests can indulge in its luxurious spa, shop in high-end boutiques, or simply relax in the grand public spaces that exude timeless glamour. The Plaza Hotel is not just a place to stay but an experience in itself, offering guests a taste of New York’s history, culture, and luxury all in one iconic location. Its unparalleled combination of history, luxury, and service makes it one of the most prestigious and beloved hotels in the world.
