Maharaja Express


The Maharajas' Express is a luxury train operated by the Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC), offering an opulent travel experience across India. Launched in 2010, it has earned international acclaim for its exceptional service, often being recognized as one of the world’s leading luxury trains. The train boasts lavish accommodations, including Deluxe Cabins, Junior Suites, Suites, and a Presidential Suite, all equipped with modern amenities. It features two elegant dining cars, **Mayur Mahal** and Rang Mahal, serving a variety of Indian and international cuisines, as well as a bar and lounge for relaxation. The Maharajas' Express offers several itineraries that cover iconic Indian destinations, such as the Golden Triangle, Varanasi, and Udaipur, along with guided tours and cultural excursions at each stop. Passengers are treated like royalty with personalized services, making the journey an indulgent exploration of India’s rich heritage.

Palace on wheel


Palace on Wheels is a luxury train in India, renowned for offering an extravagant journey through the country's most iconic destinations, reflecting the grandeur of royal travel. Launched in 1982, it was one of the first luxury trains in India and continues to attract travelers seeking a regal experience. The train features opulent interiors inspired by the personal carriages of former Indian princes, with each coach named after a princely state of Rajasthan. Guests enjoy royal treatment in well-appointed cabins equipped with modern amenities, along with two lavish dining cars, **Maharaja** and **Maharani**, which serve a variety of Indian and international cuisines. The Palace on Wheels covers popular destinations such as Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Agra, allowing passengers to explore historical landmarks, forts, palaces, and wildlife sanctuaries. Its combination of luxury, heritage, and impeccable service makes it one of the most sought-after travel experiences in India.

Golden Chariot


The Golden Chariot is a luxury train in India, operated by the Karnataka State Tourism Development Corporation (KSTDC) in collaboration with the Indian Railways. Launched in 2008, it offers travelers a luxurious journey through the southern states of Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala, exploring the region’s rich cultural heritage and scenic landscapes. The train features elegantly designed coaches named after dynasties that once ruled the southern regions, with each coach providing modern amenities and royal décor. The Golden Chariot includes two fine-dining restaurants, a bar lounge, and a spa, ensuring a blend of comfort and indulgence during the trip. Its itineraries cover a range of attractions, from the historical ruins of Hampi and the palaces of Mysore to the backwaters of Kerala and ancient temples of Tamil Nadu. Known for its combination of luxury, culture, and picturesque destinations, the Golden Chariot provides a royal way to experience the grandeur of South India.

Ramayana Express


The Ramayana Express is a special tourist train operated by the Indian Railways, offering a spiritual journey based on the epic Hindu text, the Ramayana. Launched to promote religious tourism, the train takes passengers on a pilgrimage to key locations associated with Lord Rama's life. The journey typically starts in Delhi and covers significant sites in India, including Ayodhya (the birthplace of Lord Rama), Nandigram, Sitamarhi, Varanasi, Prayagraj, Hampi, Rameswaram, and other holy places related to the Ramayana. Some itineraries even extend to Sri Lanka, allowing travelers to visit important locations like Colombo and Nuwara Eliya, which are tied to the latter part of the epic. The Ramayana Express is equipped with basic but comfortable facilities, offering a unique opportunity for devotees to immerse themselves in the spiritual and cultural significance of the Ramayana, while traveling across both India and Sri Lanka.
